Bio

I'm a Graduate Research Assistant at the Scientific Computing and Imaging Institute at the University of Utah, working with Valerio Pascucci. Before joining the Ph.D. program at Utah I obtained a B.S. in physics with a minor in computer science at the University of California, Riverside in 2014. My research interests cover a range of areas in scientific visualization and computer graphics including: distributed rendering, virtual reality, in situ visualization and ray tracing.
